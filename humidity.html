This plot applies to the Northern Hemisphere. Out of the humid cities observed, there is a relatively even distribution throughout the globe in the Northern Hemisphere. However, it bears noting that none of the driest cities are within 10 degrees of the equator, while all of the driest cities are at 20-45 latitude. There is a slight clustering of the more humid cities, though humid cities were found at all latitudes north of the equater.

The findings of the Southern Hemisphere are similar to those in the north, and it is good to note that none of the driest cities lie on the equator.
